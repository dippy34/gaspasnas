FAQ

What is Whack Your Boss?

Whack Your Boss is a dark humor point-and-click flash game developed by Tom Winkler where you find creative ways to "eliminate" your boss in a cartoon office setting.

How do you play Whack Your Boss?

In Whack Your Boss, you click on different objects around the office to trigger various animated sequences depicting the boss's demise.

Who developed Whack Your Boss?

Whack Your Boss was created by Tom Winkler and is part of the Whack Your series of casual games.

What is the main objective in Whack Your Boss?

The main objective of Whack Your Boss is to discover all the unique ways to "whack" your boss by interacting with various objects in the environment.

Is there any progression system or scoring in Whack Your Boss?

Whack Your Boss does not have a traditional progression or scoring system; the goal is simply to unlock and watch all possible attack animations.
